About

What Is Women on The Rise?​

Not Your Typical Networking —
We're bringing. Business. Faith. Mimosa's. & Fun…
to the same room

Welcome to Women on The Rise—a no-judgment, high-value networking experience for faith–based networking and sisterhood designed for women who are building bold dreams while keeping faith at the center.

 

We are a collective of entrepreneurs, professionals, creatives, and community builders rising into purpose, power, and connection. This is a place where prayer, parties & purpose collide. Where business blends with belief. Where authenticity, sisterhood, and support are the true currency.

 

No comparing. No competing. Only celebrating what each of us feel called to do.

 

We believe that when women are supported, seen, prayed for and celebrated, the whole world shifts. We meet twice monthly for connection, encouragement, prayer, and growth—with a no-judgment guarantee. You don’t have to choose between business, belief, or belonging, we bring them all to the same room.
