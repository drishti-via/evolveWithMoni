Frequently Asked Questions

What is Winning in Friendship?

Winning in Friendship is the name of The Friendship Class which is a live online  transformative class, that empowers women to pursue meaningful and lasting friendships. It offers a four-week self-development curriculum that teaches participants how to attract and nurture authentic connections.

Who is the facilitator of Winning in Friendship Class?

Winning in Friendship was created by and is facilitated by Moni Giles, a Friendship and Empowerment Coach and the founder of The R.A.R.E. Women’s Empowerment Community. She is also an event host and public speaker. Additionally, the class moderator and assistant, Sam Welsch, is a licensed therapist who provides valuable insights throughout the course.

How can the Winning in Friendship class help me?

Winning in Friendship can help you:

  • Increase your confidence through self-development tools that shifts your perspective and mindset

  • Get connected to R.A.R.E. communities & individuals that will support, empower and help you evolve

  • Learn essential social skills and knowledge to attract, maintain, and thrive in authentic, long-lasting friendships

When does Winning in Friendship start?

The 4-week sessions are held weekly online on Tuesday nights from 7 – 9 pm. Check the website for upcoming sessions. There are no sessions in the months of April, August, and December.

Are there any testimonials from previous participants?

Yes, there are many testimonials from previous participants on the website. They share their experiences of taking the class, joining the community, how it has positively impacted their lives, and/or why they recommend taking the class.

Can men join Winning in Friendship?

While Winning in Friendship primarily focuses on empowering women in their pursuit of friendships, men are welcome to join the July session and will also greatly benefit from the course.

How can I enroll in the class?

To enroll in the class, you can visit the website www.thefriendshipclass.com and find the enrollment details, including registration fees and instructions on securing your spot.

How long is Winning in Friendship?

Winning in Friendship is a four-week course, hosted on Tuesday nights for one month. With a bonus in-person Completion Party in the 5th week. Each week consists of:

  • Progressive Lessons that build on each other from week to week

  • Group & Break Out sessions

  • Activities designed to help participants become more self-aware and empowered to embrace & show up as their authentic self

  • Learning principles of friendship that we’re not taught

  • Setting and enforcing boundaries that produce healthy & aligned relationships 

  • Q&A sessions for students to get their individual friendship questions addressed.
What happens in Week 5?

Week 5 is an in-person completion party for those who are local, where they will:

  • Receive their completion gift

  • Meet members of the RARE community

  • Celebrate 🎉

 

For those who are NOT LOCAL, they will:

  • Receive their gift in the mail

  • Have the option to schedule a free 30-minute one-on-one session with Moni regarding friendship, purpose, or any other area they may need further assistance with.

What topics are covered in the class?

Topics covered in Winning in Friendship include but are not limited to:

  • The power of self-awareness & self-development

  • Becoming someone who attracts R.A.R.E. friendships (reliable, authentic, reciprocating and elevating)

  • Standard & Boundary Setting

  • Navigating the difficult terrains of friendship

  • Where to find your ideal friends

  • Each class ends with a Q&A session to ensure friendship topics meet the needs of each cohort 
What if I miss a class?

Participants can only miss one out of the four classes and still be eligible to attend the completion party. All assignments must be submitted to attend the event.

Additionally, participants must notify the facilitator at least 24 hours in advance if they will be missing a class.

What kind of materials are provided in Winning in Friendship?

Participants will receive:

  • Access to the course syllabus

  • Online resources

  • Checklists

  • Assessments & homework that deepen the understanding of self

What is the payment option for Winning in Friendship?

Payments for the class are securely processed via Stripe on the website www.thefriendshipclass.com.

What is the refund policy of Winning in Friendship?

All payments are non-refundable after the first week and non-transferable. If you feel that the program isn’t right for you after the first class, we will issue you a 100% refund.

What is the R.A.R.E. Friendship Community, and how do I join?

Taking the course makes women eligible to join the R.A.R.E. Friendship Community for:

  • $19.99 a month (can be canceled anytime)

  • $199 a year (discounted for those committed to being in community and making a long-term commitment)

This membership fee helps to cover:

  • Administrative costs

  • Operational fees

  • Venue deposits

  • Special gifts

  • Marketing costs

  • And more!

YOU DO NOT HAVE TO JOIN THE FRIENDSHIP COMMUNITY AFTER TAKING THE COURSE. 

How can I attend the Friendship Suite Retreat?

Completion of the 4-week course makes you eligible to attend The Friendship Suite Retreats. The retreats are available to those who have completed the course to ensure the foundational culture of this community is well understood before attending the intentional environment that is fostered at these retreats. 

How can I start my own R.A.R.E. Friendship Community?

Completion of the 4-week course makes you eligible to receive Community Builder Training, a program specifically designed to:

  • Train, equip, and support Community Builders

  • Help expand the mission of Happier Women, Happier World

  • Help other women launch their own version of R.A.R.E. Friendship Communities
