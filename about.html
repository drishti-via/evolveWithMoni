Moni is a lover of people, invigorating events, inspiring quotes, dancing & ice cream!

She finds great joy in connecting people to resources & communities that enrich and bring deeper fulfillment to their lives.

Moni is a fun-loving & thought-provoking public speaker, women’s empowerment fanatic, community builder, and event curator, known for her dedication to gathering, connecting, & uplifting women.
 
As the owner of Evolve with Moni LLC – a social growth & self-development business – and the Founder of the R.A.R.E. Women’s Empowerment Community, she focuses on positivity, purpose, friendship, and community as essential pillars of a woman’s overall well-being.
 

Through her public speaking, events, & community Moni creates spaces where women can authentically belong, evolve, and be inspired to fulfill all of their personal & professional goals.

She is fully dedicated to this work because she firmly believes in her business motto, Happier Women = Happier World.
 
Founder of many different connecting communities & non-profits, such as The Xcel Young Adults Ministry, The Oasis Single Sailors Program, The Bellevue High-Risers Networking Group & The R.A.R.E. Women’s Empowerment Community, Moni has over 25 years of experience in community building, social engagement, leadership, mentorship, coaching, advising, event planning & facilitation, strategic planning, public speaking,  & relationship building.
 
Come connect to learn more about this once-deathly shy, introverted, insecure, scared to be noticed, last-to-be  chosen girl who has evolved into a people-loving powerhouse of empowerment, connection, purpose, and positivity! (woah, that’s a lot p’s, lol)

Need a Speaker For Your Next Event?

Moni is a joyful, soul-stirring Speaker and engaging Emcee who instantly makes audiences feel comfortable, seen, and connected. With a radiant presence and a gift for storytelling, she speaks powerfully on the power of healthy relationships, the courage to change course, and the unshakable truth that as long as you’re alive—there’s still hope. Her heart beats for women and young adults, inspiring them to live, pursue purpose, choose positivity, and intentionally build life-giving communities. Whether hosting from the stage or delivering a keynote, Moni brings contagious joy, clarity, and belonging to every room she enters. Her message reminds us that transformation begins within & without permission.
