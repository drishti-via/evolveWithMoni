Community Event Details

Women on the Rise Networking Community

 
Evening Recharge – 1st Wednesdays at 6:30 PM
  • Alternates between business workshops and creative sessions. Open to members and guests. Included in monthly membership for members; ticketed for drop-in guests.
  • Focus: Connection, unity, growth, and support for purpose-driven women, professionals, and entrepreneurs.
Lunch Boost (Members Only) – 3rd Wednesdays at 12 PM in Bellevue
  • A powerful midday reset and check-in to fuel vision, accountability, and community support.
Quarterly Outings (Members + Plus One)
  • Intentionally fun experiences designed for bonding, joy, and deepening relationships—varies by season and activity.

 

R.A.R.E. Friendship Community Events

 
  • Friendship Meetups –  2nd Fridays or Saturdays of each month. 
    • Open to members and guests. These are connection-focused gatherings to connect women, strengthen friendships and create memories.
  • Fourth Sunday Brunch – Every 4th Sunday. Check calendar for times and locations.
    • Members-only event centered on celebration, reflection, & tightening our core community.
  • Annual Friendship Retreat – Second Weekend of September. Exclusively for Friendship class Alumni.
    • An annual time to get away to reset, reflect, renew & come back to your core with friends.
